Passing

Passingwas created by Lucah Rosenberg Lee & J. Mitchel Reed; this film taught us lessons, blossomed life long friendships and has been shown to thousands of people around the world. Our aim from the beginning was to start conversations from a place of compassion and love. More than six years later, that goal is the same. No matter who you are or what life brings along the way, it’s our human connection and mutual experiences of change, growth and self discovery that tie us together. This film is meant to ask key questions about what it means to break out of our own mental boundaries of who we think we are, who we think we should be and who we will be in our future.
